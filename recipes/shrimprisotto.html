Shrimp Risotto

Risotto is a classic Italian recipe that can be made with arborio rice or leftover rice. Shrimp risotto is simple to prepare and requires just a few ingredients: vegetable broth, parmesan cheese, lemon and white wine.

The dish is a suggestion to make in special occasions. Check out how to make it!

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of cleaned shrimps
  • salt to taste
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 drizzle of olive oil to cook the rice
  • Half chopped onion
  • 2 cups of arborio rice
  • 200 milliliters of dry white wine
  • 1 liter of boiling water
  • 1 cube of vegetable broth
  • 1 diced tomato
  • Fresh basil to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese

Steps to make it happen

  1. Season the shrimp with salt to taste, juice of half a lemon and 1 clove of chopped garlic.
  2. In a pan, add 2 tablespoons of olive oil and the shrimp. Sauté and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add 1 drizzle of olive oil and half a chopped onion. Sauté.
  4. Then add 2 cups of arborio rice and mix.
  5. Add 200 milliliters of dry white wine and mix again.
  6. In another pan, dissolve 1 vegetable stock cube in 1 liter of boiling water.
  7. Add the very hot vegetable broth, little by little and stirring constantly, to the pan with the arborio rice.
  8. Then, add the shrimp, 1 diced tomato and fresh basil to taste. Mix.
  9. Then add 1 tablespoon of cold butter, 2 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese and mix well.
  10. Serve immediately.
